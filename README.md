# jangadeiro

> There's always a partner by your side, rowing towards the same destination.

**Jangadeiro** is a collection of skills and plugins for Claude Code, created to be the perfect partner in software development — just as the rower is the indispensable partner to the master on the raft.

---

## The Metaphor

The Ceará raft is propelled by two: the **master**, who knows the currents and sets the course, and the **rower**, who propels the vessel with strength and precision.

In this relationship, the developer is the master. The skills are the rower.

Together, they go further.
